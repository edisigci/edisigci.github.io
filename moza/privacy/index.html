Moza — Privacy Policy & Support

Last updated: 2026-06-01

Privacy Policy

Moza turns your own photos into a photo mosaic. Everything happens on your device.

  • Your photos never leave your phone. Moza reads images from your photo library only to build a mosaic locally. No photo, thumbnail, or mosaic is uploaded to any server.
  • No account, no login, no tracking. Moza does not require an account and does not collect personal information.
  • No third-party analytics or advertising SDKs collect your data for cross-app tracking.
  • Purchases are handled by Apple's App Store / StoreKit. Apple processes payment; Moza never sees your payment details.
  • Diagnostics, if any, are limited to anonymous, aggregate crash/stability data and contain no photos or personal content.

Because processing is fully on-device, deleting the app removes all associated data.
